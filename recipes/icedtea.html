Iced-tea recipe here.

Description

Iced tea (or ice tea) is a form of cold tea. Though usually served in a glass with ice, it can refer to any tea that has been chilled or cooled. It may be sweetened with sugar, syrup and/or apple slices. Iced tea is also a popular packaged drink and can be mixed with flavored syrup, with multiple common flavors including lemon, raspberry, lime, passion fruit, peach, orange, strawberry, and cherry.

Ingredients

  • 2-1/4 cups water.
  • 6 regular-size tea bags (about 1/2-ounce total), or 2 family-size tea bags.
  • 6 cups cold water.
  • Granulated sugar for garnish.
  • Lemon wedges for garnish.
  • Mint sprigs for garnish.

Steps to create Iced-tea(lemon).

  1. In a pan pour 1.25 litres water.
  2. Heat the pan on a medium flame and let the water come to a boil.
  3. Once the water comes to a boil, switch off the flame and add 2 tablespoons black tea powder. For a light tea taste, you can add 1 tablespoon tea powder. Instead of tea powder, you can also use black or green tea leaves.
  4. Add ⅓ to ½ cup sugar. For a less sweet taste, you can add ¼ cup sugar.
  5. Mix very well and let the tea leaves steep in the water for 4 to 5 minutes. For a lighter tea flavor, you can steep for 2 to 3 minutes.
  6. After the tea is steeped in the hot water, then add 3 tablespoons of lemon juice. For a more lemony taste, you can add 4 tablespoons of lemon juice also. You can also skip the lemon juice altogether and make a simple iced tea.
  7. Stir with a spoon.
  8. Then strain the tea in a steel or glass jar. The glass jar should be able to handle the heat.
  9. While serving, add 4 to 5 ice cubes in a glass. You can add less or more as per your preference. Also, add 1 to 2 lemon slices. Adding lemon slices is optional.
