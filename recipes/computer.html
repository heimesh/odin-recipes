A Computer making recipe

Description

Assembling a computer is not as hard as it seems. Though making one is quite tricky. Here we break down how to create a computer for dummies.

Componenets required

  • A Cabinet.
  • A Motherboard.
  • A CPU chip (Ryzen/Intel).
  • An SDD or HDD.
  • A RAM stick (Two 8/16GB preferred).
  • Power Supply.
  • Cooling system (Fans/Liquid).
  • A rare GPU card.

Building your first wallet ripper!

  1. Take your motherboard and slap on some anti-static wrist band.
  2. Put down your chip slowly onto the slot and click it in with the help of the little lever on the side. Don't put too much pressure. Make sure it fits.
  3. Slap on that heavy GPU in the slot.
  4. Take a breath. You are almost there.
  5. Grab those RAM sticks and plug each into it's own channel. Make sure it's the right way in before you smash it, or it can break.
  6. Get your SDD/HDD and screw it in.
  7. Install the montherboard in the cabinet.
  8. Put on some thermal paste and stick in your cooling system.
  9. Now beautifully arrange those wires.
  10. Attach the proper power supply unit.
  11. *Breathing intensifies* Now plug in your periphirals and boot it up. If it's all good, screw it up.
  12. If you messed it up, well. Learn how to google like a pro.
